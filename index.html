What is Etsy?

Discover our wonderful story

Welcome to Etsy!

Welcome to Etsy, the online marketplace where you can buy directly from passionate sellers.

A platform celebrating artistic expression and creativity Here, makers and crafters from all over the world gather to share their creations with over 88 million passionate buyers. Crafts, vintage decor, giftable accessories and handmade clothes: Etsy is the perfect place to discover extraordinary items, shop local, and support the craftsmanship of independent sellers.

Why Etsy?

Are you looking for unique treasures, extraordinary pieces or customizable gifts? Etsy is the ideal place to bring your ideas and dreams to life thanks to its infinite selection of items. With more than 100 million items listed, we offer a wide range of products suitable for all budgets.

On Etsy, you will find something for every person and occasion, from home items and furniture to clothing and accessories: rugs, cocktail glasses, accessories for children, personalized jewelry, dresses, prints, and custom portraits...

What will you set your heart on? An affordable treat or a superb piece for your home? Support small businesses while also buying items which are both sustainable and planet-friendly. You will no doubt find something you love in our wide selection of exceptional items created on a small scale and expertly designed.

Buy and sell the extraordinary We value both the individuality and creativity of sellers, French or otherwise, who put all of their passion and craftsmanship into every item they create. A superb wall decor gift to place under the Christmas tree, a custom piece of jewelry to offer a loved one for their birthday, a toy to celebrate the birth of a baby, or a handmade piece of clothing just for you… Each order reflects the love and care of a person full of passion.

Keep commerce human Each order you place on Etsy is much more than a simple transaction. You support know-how, local craftsmanship and creative businesses, while also enjoying a personalized and friendly experience. Together with our 5.9 million Etsy sellers, we strive to create a buyer-seller experience with a human connection.

By exploring the different shops on Etsy, you can discover and buy unique items which are created locally and are the direct product of French craftsmanship.

Why trust Etsy?

Our site is well-known for being reliable and trustworthy for secure online shopping.

Well-established reputation: The Etsy platform was created in 2005, and over the years we have acquired a strong reputation with millions of French buyers.

Seller verification: Before they are allowed to sell on our platform, Etsy sellers must provide verifiable information and comply with our quality and integrity standards.

Reviews and feedback: Buyers can leave feedback and reviews after their purchases. These reviews give an insight into the buying experience of other people and help make an informed decision before placing an order.

Buyer protection: We provide a guarantee of buyer protection to ensure you are satisfied with your purchase. In case of a problem with your order, you can contact our customer service team and get help.

Secure payments: Etsy offers various secure payment options, including reputable third-party payment providers. Your payment information is processed securely in order to guarantee the privacy of your data.

Tracked shipping: We offer secure and tracked shipping of your order through various shipping carriers.

Join our community and discover treasures on Etsy today. Get inspiration from our blog, where you will find uplifting stories from our community as well as shopping ideas.

How do I become an Etsy seller?

The good news is: Our platform is open to everyone! As long as your products comply with Etsy's policies, you are eligible to sell your creations. Whether you are an independent crafts person, a creator of unique products, a reseller of vintage products or even a small business, in order to sell on Etsy, you must follow a few simple steps:

1) Create a seller account: TStart by creating an Etsy account (if you don’t already have one) and set up your Etsy shop.

2) Manage your listings: Let's go! You can create listings for each item that you wish to sell.

3) Process orders and shipping: You can choose between several shipping and tracking options in order to meet the needs of your customers.

4) Take care of your buyers: Answer their queries, provide updates on their orders, and offer outstanding customer service.

Secure payments: Etsy offers various secure payment options, including reputable third-party payment providers. Your payment information is processed securely in order to guarantee the privacy of your data.

You are on the road to success!
